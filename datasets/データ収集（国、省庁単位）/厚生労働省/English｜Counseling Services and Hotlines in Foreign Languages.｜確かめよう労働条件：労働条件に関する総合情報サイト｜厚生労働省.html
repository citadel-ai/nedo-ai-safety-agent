Telephone Consultation Service for Foreign Workers

You may call any of the following numbers in thirteen different languages to get an advice on working conditions.(The number will connect you to Telephone Consultation Service for Foreign Workers.) When you call the Telephone Consultation Service for Foreign Workers, you will be charged 8.5 yen (incl. tax) per 180 seconds from landlines, or 10 yen (incl. tax) per 20 seconds from cellphones.
There might be a change in the available days and hours.

10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.(closed between noon to 1:00 p.m.)

Labour Standards Advice Hotline

The Labour Standards Advice Hotline is a commissioned service entrusted by the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare. The hotline offers free general advice on working conditions from anywhere in Japan during the hours when the Prefectural Labour Bureaus and Labour Standards Inspection Offices are closed or in weekends and holidays. Foreign language support is also available.

Hours：
Weekdays (Monday to Friday) 5:00 p.m to 10:00 p.m.Saturday, Sunday, holidays 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m.
